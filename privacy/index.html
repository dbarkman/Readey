Privacy Policy

Readey, the iOS App, and speedReadey.com, the website, both adhere to this very simple and straightforward Privacy Policy

We will not, under any circumstances whatsoever, give out or sell your information (including your name and email address) to anyone. Period.

We will also never send you an email unless you email us first or provide your email in a feedback or support form.

Also, no one will ever have access to your saved articles, or any other data. The only exceptions to this rule:

  • From time to time, a developer will have to access source code in order to update the app or website, perform routine maintenance, and possibly fix bugs. However, all your data will still remain 100% confidential.
  • It's possible that we will be required by subpoena or other legal action to grant access to an individual in the process of an investigation.
